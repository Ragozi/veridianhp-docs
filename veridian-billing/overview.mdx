# Veridian Billing

<Note>
  **On hold.** Veridian Billing is deprioritized. All engineering focus is
  on the [Veridian Health](/veridian-health/overview) MVP. This page exists
  as a placeholder only.
</Note>

Veridian Billing was planned as medical billing and coding infrastructure
for correctional and community health programs — a future product under
[veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com).

There is **no active development timeline**. When Veridian Health reaches
production and partner demand warrants it, billing may be revisited.

For current product documentation, see [Veridian Health](/veridian-health/overview).
