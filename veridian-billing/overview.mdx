# Veridian Billing

<Note>
  **Future product — no timeline.** Veridian Billing is planned as a second
  product under [veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com). All current
  engineering focus is on delivering the **Veridian Health MVP** (AI-assisted
  PT assessments for inmates). This page will expand when billing development
  begins.
</Note>

Veridian Billing will provide **medical billing and coding infrastructure**
for correctional and community health programs — packaged alongside Veridian
Health and future products from Veridian Health Partners.

## What we're planning

| | Veridian Health (now) | Veridian Billing (future) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Focus** | AI-assisted PT assessments | Revenue cycle (claims, coding) |
| **Primary users** | Jails, medical staff, inmates | Billing teams, integrators |
| **Status** | MVP in development | Not yet scheduled |

Planned capabilities when development starts:

- Coding and claim generation from clinical encounters
- API-first integration with facility systems
- HIPAA-aligned processing and audit trails
- Webhook-driven claim status events

## Shared infrastructure

Veridian Billing will share the Veridian Health Partners foundation:

- HIPAA-aligned security and BAA framework
- Unified account experience at [veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com)
- Common audit logging and incident response

See [Security overview](/security/overview) for our current compliance posture.

## Stay updated

Watch the [changelog](/changelog) for announcements. For now, see
