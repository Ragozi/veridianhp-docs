# Quickstart

This guide walks jail teams through the path from "we want PT access" to a
completed AI-assisted assessment with PT-driven recommendations. Timelines
vary by jail size and compliance review; most pilots go live within a few
weeks of signing agreements.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Start at [veridianhp.com/request-access](https://veridianhp.com/request-access).
    Tell us your jail type, approximate inmate population, and current PT
    coverage. Our team schedules an intro call within a few business days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Execute the BAA, provision admin and medical staff accounts, approve
    Veridian-enrolled providers for your jail, and set up assessment devices
    per the setup checklist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your first assessment device">
    Designate a secure location with a facility-controlled tablet or
    workstation with a working camera. Confirm network connectivity. Run a
    test assessment with Veridian support using synthetic data before
    scheduling real inmate sessions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first referral">
    Medical staff submit a referral for an inmate who needs PT assessment.
    Include chief complaint, relevant history, and facility constraints.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run your first assessment">
    Escort the inmate to the device. They complete four guided photos (front,
    back, left, right) and the tailored questionnaire. The platform generates
    PT-driven recommendations for medical staff to review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and expand">
    After pilot assessments, meet with the Veridian team to review workflow,
    recommendation quality, and staff feedback. Expand referral criteria and
    housing units as ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Checklist before your first inmate assessment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Agreements and compliance">
    - [ ] BAA signed between jail and Veridian Health Partners
    - [ ] Jail healthcare policies reviewed and aligned
    - [ ] Staff trained on referral and assessment workflows
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Technical setup">
    - [ ] Assessment device provisioned with working camera and network
    - [ ] Admin and medical staff accounts active
    - [ ] Test assessment completed with Veridian support
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Provider and operational readiness">
    - [ ] At least one PT enrolled in Veridian network and approved by your jail
    - [ ] Custody staff briefed on escort procedures
    - [ ] Medical unit knows how to create referrals and review recommendations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pilot environment

Veridian Health provides a **pilot environment** for jails entering early
access. Pilot environments use synthetic or de-identified test data — no
real inmate PHI — so staff can practice the photo and questionnaire flow
before production go-live.

Contact your Veridian account contact to request pilot access after
onboarding begins.

## You're done

Once your first assessment completes and medical staff review the
recommendations, the full loop is working: **referral → photos →
questionnaire → AI analysis → PT-driven recommendations → facility action**.
Everything after that is scaling.
