# Veridian Health

Veridian Health gives jails a scalable way to deliver **physical therapy
guidance** to inmates without on-site PT staff or off-site transport. The
MVP is an **AI-assisted assessment**: the inmate captures four guided photos
and completes a tailored questionnaire; the platform produces **PT-driven
recommendations** the facility medical team can act on.

## The problem

Jail healthcare operates under constraints community clinics don't face:

- **On-site PT is rare.** Most jails have no dedicated physical therapist;
  musculoskeletal complaints pile up in nursing queues.
- **Transport is expensive.** Off-site specialist visits require security
  escorts, scheduling, and liability jails can't scale.
- **Inmates can't self-refer.** Access depends entirely on facility
  workflow — any friction is a direct barrier to care.
- **Documentation gaps.** Without structured assessment, complaints get
  treated symptomatically and chronic conditions worsen.

## What Veridian Health provides (MVP)

The first release focuses on one workflow, done well:

1. **Guided photo capture** — four images (left, right, front, back) taken
   on a facility-controlled device with on-screen guidance for consistent
   positioning.
2. **Tailored questionnaire** — dynamic questions based on the inmate's
   chief complaint, injury history, pain location, and mobility status.
3. **AI-assisted analysis** — the platform evaluates photos and responses
   against PT clinical frameworks.
4. **PT-driven recommendations** — structured output including assessment
   summary, exercise and mobility guidance adapted for the custody
   environment, red-flag alerts, and follow-up suggestions.

<Note>
  The MVP is **assessment and recommendations**, not live video consults.
  Live PT sessions and deeper EHR integration are on the roadmap after the
  core workflow is proven in production jails.
</Note>

## Who it's for

**Jails** — any correctional facility that purchases Veridian Health. County
jails, regional detention centers, and other custody settings with inmate
healthcare programs.

**Approved providers** — licensed physical therapists enrolled in Veridian's
provider network **and approved by the purchasing jail system** before they
can review cases or contribute to clinical oversight.

**Inmates (patients)** — individuals referred by facility medical staff who
complete the assessment and receive PT-driven care guidance.

## What the facility gets

- Faster PT assessment without adding headcount
- Structured recommendations nursing and medical staff can implement
- Audit trail of every assessment, photo, and recommendation
- HIPAA-aligned handling of inmate health data
