# Veridian Health

Veridian Health connects inmates in correctional facilities with licensed
physical therapists through a secure, facility-integrated consult platform.

Our first focus is **physical therapy** — one of the most deferred specialty
services in custody settings. Inmates with musculoskeletal injuries, chronic
pain, post-surgical needs, and mobility limitations often wait months for
on-site PT or never receive it at all. Veridian Health closes that gap with
remote consults that fit inside existing facility workflows.

## The problem

Correctional healthcare operates under constraints that community clinics don't:

- **Transport and security** make every off-site specialist visit expensive
  and slow to schedule.
- **On-site PT staffing** is inconsistent — many facilities rely on
  contract rotation or have no PT coverage at all.
- **Documentation and handoffs** between custody staff, nursing, and
  external providers create gaps in continuity of care.
- **Inmates can't self-refer.** Access depends entirely on facility
  processes, making friction in those processes a direct barrier to care.

The result: treatable conditions become chronic, functional decline
accelerates, and facilities absorb higher long-term medical and legal risk.

## What Veridian Health provides

Veridian Health is a platform — not a staffing agency — that gives facilities
and providers the tools to deliver PT consults inside the facility:

- **Secure telehealth consults** between inmates and licensed physical
  therapists, conducted on facility-controlled devices or designated
  endpoints.
- **Structured intake and scheduling** so medical unit staff can refer,
  queue, and track consults without ad-hoc phone calls or fax workflows.
- **Clinical documentation** that produces visit notes, treatment
  recommendations, and follow-up plans facilities can act on.
- **Compliance by default** — HIPAA-aligned PHI handling, audit trails, and
  role-based access designed for correctional environments.

## Who it's for

**Correctional facilities** — county jails, state prisons, and detention
centers that need scalable PT access without expanding on-site headcount or
transport budgets.

**Licensed physical therapists** — clinicians who want to deliver care in
correctional settings through a purpose-built platform, not improvised
video calls and unsecured email.

**Inmates (patients)** — individuals who receive timely PT assessment and
guidance as part of their facility healthcare program.

## What we're building first

Veridian Health launches with a focused scope:

1. **Facility onboarding** — staff roles, inmate enrollment, device setup
2. **Consult scheduling and intake** — referral → queue → session
3. **Live PT consult** — assessment, exercise guidance, care planning
4. **Documentation and follow-up** — visit notes, recommendations, re-referral

Additional specialties and deeper EHR integrations come after PT is
proven in production.
