# For facilities

This page covers what correctional facility staff — administrators, medical
units, and operations teams — need to know to bring Veridian Health online
and run it day to day.

## What you get

Veridian Health gives your facility access to licensed physical therapists
without adding on-site headcount or coordinating off-site transport for every
consult. Your medical unit refers inmates who need PT; our platform connects
them to a credentialed provider for a secure remote consult, with
documentation your team can act on immediately.

## Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Submit a [request access](https://veridianhp.com/request-access) form.
    Our team schedules an intro call to understand your facility type,
    approximate population, current PT coverage, and compliance requirements.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Execute agreements">
    Before any PHI flows, your facility signs a **Business Associate
    Agreement (BAA)** with Veridian Health Partners. We provide a standard
    BAA aligned with HIPAA requirements; legal teams can review and redline
    as needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your organization">
    A Veridian administrator provisions your facility: organization profile,
    user accounts, role assignments, and consult location/device settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up consult endpoints">
    Designate one or more secure locations with facility-controlled devices
    (tablets or workstations) for inmate consults. Devices must meet your
    facility's network and security policies; our team provides a setup
    checklist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Train your staff">
    Medical unit and custody staff receive orientation on the referral
    workflow, inmate preparation, session monitoring, and post-consult
    documentation access. Training materials are provided in-platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pilot and expand">
    Most facilities start with a pilot cohort — a defined housing unit or
    referral type — before rolling out facility-wide. We support you through
    the pilot review before general availability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Staff roles

| Role | Access | Typical actions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Administrator** | Full org settings | User management, policy config, reporting |
| **Medical staff** | Referrals, scheduling, notes | Create referrals, view consult notes |
| **Custody staff** | Session logistics (optional) | View scheduled sessions, escort coordination |
| **Read-only / audit** | Reports and logs | Compliance review, no clinical actions |

Role assignments follow least-privilege: custody staff see only what they
need for escort and monitoring; clinical notes are restricted to authorized
medical personnel.

## Day-to-day workflow

### Creating a referral

Medical staff identify an inmate who would benefit from PT and submit a
referral through the Veridian Health dashboard. The referral includes:

- Inmate identifier (per your facility's naming convention)
- Chief complaint and relevant history
- Urgency level
- Any constraints (housing unit, mobility, security level)

Veridian Health collects the **minimum necessary** PHI for the consult. See
[data handling](/security/data-handling) for details on what we store.

### Preparing for a session

When a consult is scheduled:

1. Medical staff notify custody of the session time and location.
2. The inmate is escorted to the designated consult device.
3. Staff confirm the device is connected and the session link is active.
4. Custody monitors the session per facility policy (line-of-sight,
   duration limits, etc.).

### After the consult

The PT's visit note is available to authorized medical staff as soon as
the session is completed. Notes include assessment findings, exercise and
mobility recommendations adapted for the custody environment, and follow-up
plan. Medical staff action items may include:

- Scheduling a follow-up consult
- Communicating exercise instructions to nursing or recreation staff
- Referring for in-person evaluation if telehealth scope is insufficient
- Updating the inmate's health record per facility protocol

## Facility requirements

| Requirement | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Network** | Stable internet at consult endpoints; outbound HTTPS required |
| **Devices** | Facility-owned tablets or workstations; no inmate personal devices |
| **Staffing** | Medical unit staff to create referrals; custody for escort as required |
| **Policies** | Existing healthcare and telehealth policies; we align to yours |
| **BAA** | Required before production use |

## Reporting and audit

Administrators can export consult activity, referral volumes, session
completion rates, and audit logs from the dashboard. Audit logs cover user
access, referral creation, session events, and documentation views — suitable
for compliance review and accreditation documentation.
