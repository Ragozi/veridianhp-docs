# FAQ

## What is Veridian Health Partners?

Veridian Health Partners is the company behind
[veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com). We build healthcare infrastructure
for jails and correctional settings. **Veridian Health** (AI-assisted PT
assessments) is our current focus. **Veridian Billing** is a future product
with no timeline yet.

## Who can use Veridian Health?

Any jail that purchases the product. County jails, regional detention
centers, and other correctional facilities with inmate healthcare programs.

## How does the assessment work?

The inmate completes **four guided photos** (front, back, left, right) and a
**tailored questionnaire** on a facility-controlled device. Veridian Health
runs AI-assisted analysis and returns **PT-driven recommendations** — exercise
guidance, activity modifications, red-flag alerts, and follow-up suggestions.

## Is this a live video consult with a PT?

Not in the MVP. The first release is AI-assisted assessment and
recommendations. Live PT consults are a future capability.

## Do inmates need their own accounts?

No. Inmates complete assessments on facility-controlled devices, escorted
and monitored per jail policy. There is no inmate account signup.

## How do providers get involved?

Licensed PTs enroll in **Veridian's provider network** (credentialing,
training, background screening). Each **jail must separately approve**
providers before they participate in that facility's workflow. Both steps are
required.

## Can we run Veridian Health without signing a BAA?

Pilot environments use synthetic or de-identified data — no BAA required.
Production use with real inmate PHI requires a signed BAA before go-live.

## What devices do we need?

Facility-owned tablets or workstations with a camera in a designated
assessment location. Inmate personal phones are not supported.

## What about the photos — is that PHI?

Yes. Assessment images and questionnaire responses are treated as PHI and
handled under HIPAA. See [data handling](/security/data-handling).

## Where is patient data stored?

In the United States, in HIPAA-aligned infrastructure. The sub-processor
list is available to jails on request.

## When will Veridian Billing be available?

There is no timeline. All current focus is on the Veridian Health MVP.
See [Veridian Billing overview](/veridian-billing/overview).

## How do I contact support?

[support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) for general support.
[security@veridianhp.com](mailto:security@veridianhp.com) for security
