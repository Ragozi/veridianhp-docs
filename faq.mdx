# FAQ

<Note>
  **Early-stage documentation.** Answers reflect current product direction
  and may be updated as the platform evolves.
</Note>

## What is Veridian Health Partners?

The company behind [veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com). We build
healthcare technology for jails and correctional facilities. **Veridian
Health** is our active product. **Veridian Billing** is on hold.

## What is Veridian Health?

A corrections-focused **posture and musculoskeletal triage platform** with
two surfaces: an **inmate kiosk** (photo capture + questionnaire) and a
**provider dashboard** (PT case review and recommendations).

## How does the inmate kiosk work?

An inmate referred by medical staff completes four guided photos (front,
back, left, right) and a tailored questionnaire on a facility-controlled
device. See [Inmate kiosk flow](/veridian-health/inmate-kiosk-flow).

## Is this a live video consult?

Not in MVP. The first release is asynchronous — kiosk intake followed by
provider dashboard review. Live consults are on the
[roadmap](/veridian-health/future-roadmap).

## How do providers get access?

PTs enroll in Veridian's network (credentialing, training, screening) and
must be **approved by each jail** separately. See
[Provider dashboard](/veridian-health/provider-dashboard).

## Who can purchase Veridian Health?

Any jail or correctional facility that buys into the product.

## Do inmates need accounts?

No. Inmates use facility-controlled kiosk devices under custody supervision.

## Is assessment photo data PHI?

Yes. Images and questionnaire responses are PHI, handled under HIPAA. See
[Data model & privacy](/veridian-health/data-model-privacy).

## What about Veridian Billing?

On hold — no active development. See
[Veridian Billing overview](/veridian-billing/overview).
