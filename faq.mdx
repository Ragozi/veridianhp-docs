# FAQ

## What is Veridian Health Partners?

The company behind [veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com). We build
healthcare technology for jails and correctional facilities. **Veridian
Motion** is our flagship PT product (formerly Veridian Health / PostureCare).

## What is Veridian Motion?

Veridian Health Partners' **flagship physical therapy platform** for
correctional facilities — structured MSK intake on an inmate kiosk plus PT
review on a provider dashboard. Start at
[Veridian Motion](/veridian-motion/product) or
[For evaluators](/veridian-motion/for-evaluators).

## Does Veridian Motion include billing or EHR integration?

**No.** The current release is a clinical workflow product — kiosk intake,
PT review, exercise plans, messaging, and PDF export. It does **not** include
medical billing, EHR/EMR integration, live video, or partner API connectivity.
Those may be considered in future phases.

## How do we roll out at a new facility?

Follow the [Getting started](/veridian-motion/getting-started) checklist:
BAA, hosting posture, kiosk/MDM setup, staff MFA, role grants, first
enrollment, and first PT review.

## How do inmates sign in?

Inmates do **not** self-register. Staff enroll each patient and hand over an
**inmate number** and **initial PIN**. See
[Inmate kiosk flow](/veridian-motion/inmate-kiosk-flow).

## Do inmates see AI results immediately after submit?

No. After submit, the inmate sees only **confirmation** and safety guidance.
AI output appears only in the staff dashboard until a PT marks the case
**reviewed**. See [Status and workflow](/veridian-motion/status-and-workflow).

## How do staff sign in?

Staff use **email, password, and TOTP MFA**. Roles are granted only by an
admin. See [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access).

## What can each staff role do?

See the [Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions) matrix. PTs
review assigned cases; admins enroll and assign; officers and wardens have
scoped operational visibility; IT admins manage security settings.

## Is this a live video consult?

No. Veridian Motion is **asynchronous** — kiosk intake, dashboard review, and
[secure messaging](/veridian-motion/messaging) inside the app only.

## Who can purchase Veridian Motion?

Jails and correctional facilities that contract with Veridian Health Partners.

## Is assessment data PHI?

Yes. See [Data model & privacy](/veridian-motion/data-model-privacy).

## Is Veridian Motion HIPAA-certified?

**No.** HIPAA-aligned design with BAA required for production PHI. CJIS,
FedRAMP, MDM, and physical controls are **agency-provided**. See
[Security overview](/security/overview).

## Does the app diagnose inmates?

No. AI output is **non-diagnostic triage support**. PTs retain clinical
authority. See [AI and clinical safety](/veridian-motion/ai-and-clinical-safety).

## Can inmates browse the web from the kiosk?

No. App hardening plus agency **MDM** required. See
[Deployment and kiosk](/veridian-motion/deployment-and-kiosk).
