# FAQ

## What is Veridian Health Partners?

Veridian Health Partners is the company. We build healthcare infrastructure
for correctional settings. **Veridian Health** (PT consults for inmates) is
our first product. **Veridian Billing** (medical billing and coding API) is
on the roadmap.

## Who can use Veridian Health?

Correctional facilities — county jails, state prisons, detention centers —
that need scalable physical therapy access for inmates. Licensed PTs who
want to deliver care in correctional settings can join our provider network.

## Do inmates need their own accounts?

No. Inmates participate in consults on facility-controlled devices, escorted
and monitored per facility policy. There is no inmate-facing app or account
signup.

## Is this telehealth?

Yes. Veridian Health delivers PT consults via secure telehealth on
facility hardware. It is not a replacement for all in-person care — cases
requiring hands-on intervention are referred for on-site evaluation.

## What specialties beyond PT are planned?

Veridian Health launches with physical therapy. Additional specialties
(behavioral health, other consult types) may follow based on facility demand
and regulatory review. Veridian Billing will address revenue cycle separately.

## Can we run Veridian Health without signing a BAA?

Pilot and sandbox environments use synthetic or de-identified data — no BAA
required. Production use with real inmate PHI requires a signed BAA before
go-live. This is non-negotiable under HIPAA.

## What devices do we need?

Facility-owned tablets or workstations in a designated consult location.
Inmate personal phones and devices are not supported. Our team provides a
setup checklist during onboarding.

## How are providers credentialed?

Licensed PTs complete licensure verification, malpractice coverage
confirmation, background screening, and platform orientation before
accepting consults.

## Where is patient data stored?

In the United States, in HIPAA-aligned infrastructure. The sub-processor
list is available to facilities on request.

## Can we export our data?

Yes. Facilities can export consult records, audit logs, and account data
from the dashboard at any time. Export is also available during offboarding.

## When will Veridian Billing be available?

Veridian Billing is in planning, sequenced after Veridian Health reaches
production. See the [Veridian Billing overview](/veridian-billing/overview)
and [changelog](/changelog) for updates.
