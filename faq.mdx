# FAQ

## What is Veridian Motion?

Veridian Health Partners' **flagship PT platform** for correctional facilities —
patient portal (kiosk) plus provider dashboard. See
[Veridian Motion](/veridian-motion/product).

## How are patients enrolled?

**Manually**, one at a time, by an **admin** for a **single-facility**
deployment. Bulk CSV import and JMS sync are **planned** —
[Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

## How do patients sign in?

**Patient ID + PIN** (4–12 digits), staff-provisioned. No self sign-up. See
[Patient portal](/veridian-motion/patient-portal).

## Do patients see AI results immediately?

No. Confirmation and safety guidance only until a PT **approves**. AI generates
a PT-reviewable plan in the dashboard immediately after processing.

## How do staff sign in?

Local **email + password**; **TOTP MFA** available, **enforced per deployment**.
SSO is **planned** — [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

## What staff roles exist?

`pt`, `admin`, `warden`, and `it_admin`. **`warden`** is operational oversight only
(enrollment counts, activity, safety escalations) — **no clinical PHI**. See
[Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions).

## Does Veridian Motion support multiple facilities?

Yes. Each facility's patients and staff are isolated via **row-level security**
scoped by facility membership. Multi-facility staff can **switch facilities** before
working in the dashboard. See [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access).

## Is live video, EHR, or API included?

No. **Planned** or future — [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

## Is Veridian Motion HIPAA-certified?

**No.** **HIPAA-aligned, not HIPAA-certified.** **Not CJIS-authorized or
FedRAMP-authorized.** Agency provides hosting, MDM, network, personnel screening.
[Security overview](/security/overview).

## Does the app diagnose patients?

No. Non-diagnostic triage; PT approves all plans. Red flags show **see medical
staff** — not diagnosis. [AI and clinical safety](/veridian-motion/ai-and-clinical-safety).
