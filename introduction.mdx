## Why correctional healthcare

Inmates have the same right to healthcare as anyone else. In practice,
delivering it inside a jail is hard:

- **Specialist access is scarce.** Physical therapy and other specialty care
  often require transport, security escorts, and scheduling jails can't
  sustain.
- **Staffing gaps persist.** On-site clinical teams are stretched thin; PT
  coverage is frequently deferred entirely.
- **Compliance burden is high.** PHI, custody rules, consent workflows, and
  audit requirements add friction to every clinical interaction.

Veridian Health Partners exists to remove that friction — starting with
physical therapy.
