# Veridian Health Partners

Veridian Health Partners builds technology that brings quality healthcare
into correctional settings — where access is limited, compliance is
non-negotiable, and the people who need care often have the fewest options.

We operate as a **product family** under one roof. Each product solves a
distinct problem for facilities, clinicians, and the patients in their care.
Shared security, compliance, and infrastructure run across all of them.

## Why correctional healthcare

Inmates have the same right to healthcare as anyone else. In practice,
delivering it inside a facility is hard:

- **Specialist access is scarce.** Physical therapy, behavioral health, and
  other specialty care often require transport, security escorts, and
  scheduling that facilities can't sustain at scale.
- **Staffing gaps persist.** On-site clinical teams are stretched thin; PT
  coverage is frequently outsourced or deferred entirely.
- **Compliance burden is high.** PHI, custody rules, consent workflows, and
  audit requirements add friction to every clinical interaction.

Veridian Health Partners exists to remove that friction — one product at a
time — starting with physical therapy.
