# Veridian Health Partners

Veridian helps small behavioral and mental health practices get paid — faster,
with less friction for patients, and without taking on PCI or PHI complexity
themselves.

Our flagship product, **Veridian Bridge**, is a hosted patient payment flow that
lets a practice send a single link or text to a patient. The patient sees a
practice-branded payment page, connects their bank in seconds, and pays by ACH.
The practice gets paid, the EHR gets updated, and the patient gets a receipt —
no card data, no portals, no calls.

## What problem we solve

Behavioral health practices lose **20–40% of patient balances** to the friction
of collecting them. Mailed statements get ignored. Card-on-file processors are
expensive. Patient portals require yet another login. Front-desk staff burn
hours chasing balances that never arrive.

Veridian removes that friction:

- **One link, one tap, one payment.** No accounts, no card numbers, no portal.
- **ACH-first.** Bank-direct payments at a fraction of card processing costs.
- **EHR-aware.** Your practice management software stays the source of truth;
  we just collect.
- **HIPAA-grade by default.** Encrypted PHI at rest, audited access, BAA-covered
  payment processing.

## Who Veridian is for

Veridian is built for **small to mid-sized behavioral and mental health
practices** (1–25 providers) that:

- Bill patients for copays, coinsurance, deductibles, and out-of-network balances
- Want to reduce A/R days and stop chasing payments manually
- Need HIPAA-grade handling of patient and visit data
- Don't want to build or maintain their own payment infrastructure

If that's you, [request access](https://veridianhp.com/request-access) and
we'll get you into the sandbox in a day.
