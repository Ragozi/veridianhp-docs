# Veridian Health Partners

<Note>
  **Early-stage documentation.** This site is actively maintained as we build
  Veridian Health. Content, workflows, and technical details will evolve as
  the product matures. Last major update: July 2026.
</Note>

Veridian Health Partners builds healthcare technology for **jails and
correctional facilities** — environments where specialist access is limited,
compliance is mandatory, and musculoskeletal complaints often go unaddressed.

Our current focus is **Veridian Health**: a corrections-focused **posture and
musculoskeletal triage platform** with two primary surfaces:

- **Inmate kiosk** — facility-controlled intake where inmates complete guided
  photo capture and a tailored questionnaire
- **Provider dashboard** — where licensed PTs review cases and deliver
  PT-driven recommendations

Everything is packaged under **[veridianhp.com](https://veridianhp.com)**,
which will serve as the hub for Veridian Health and future products as they
launch.

## Why we exist

Jails face a consistent gap in musculoskeletal care:

- On-site physical therapy is rare; nursing staff handle MSK complaints without specialist support
- Off-site referrals require transport, security escorts, and scheduling jails cannot scale
- Without structured triage, treatable conditions become chronic and documentation suffers

Veridian Health closes that gap with a workflow designed specifically for
custody settings — not a generic telehealth tool adapted after the fact.
