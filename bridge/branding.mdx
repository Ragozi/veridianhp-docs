# Branding the Bridge

The patient-facing Bridge is hosted by Veridian but should feel like your
practice. This page covers what you can customize.

## What you control

- **Logo.** PNG or SVG, ideally with a transparent background.
- **Practice display name.** The name that appears across the page.
- **Primary color.** The accent color used on the pay button and progress
  indicators. We recommend a color that contrasts well on both light and
  dark mode.
- **Support contact.** A phone number or email shown if the patient needs help.
- **Refund / billing notes.** Optional fine print displayed under the amount.

## What Veridian controls

- **Layout.** We test the layout against accessibility, mobile usability,
  and conversion. Practices don't customize the layout — we improve it for
  everyone.
- **Bank-connection flow.** Provided by our partner; not customizable.
- **Receipt template.** Branded with your logo and name, but the structure
  is fixed for clarity and audit.
- **Security indicators.** Lock icons, "secured by Veridian" language, and
  HIPAA references stay consistent across all practices.

## How to update branding

Sign in to the [Veridian dashboard](https://app.veridianhp.com), open
**Settings → Branding**, and upload your assets. Changes are live within
a few minutes.

## Best practices

- Use a logo that reads cleanly at 40px tall on mobile.
- Pick a primary color that has at least 4.5:1 contrast against white.
- Keep support contact info current — patients will use it.
- Test the page on your own phone before sending it to a real patient.
