# Troubleshooting

Most issues fall into a few categories: access and accounts, inmate kiosk
setup, photo capture, and provider dashboard. Start with the symptom that
matches your situation.

## Access and accounts

**I can't log in to the dashboard.**
- Confirm you're using the email address provisioned during onboarding.
- Reset your password from the login page.
- Contact [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) if the
  issue persists.

**A staff member can't see recommendations.**
- Check their role — clinical output requires medical staff or administrator
  access.
- Administrators adjust roles in **Settings → Users**.

**A provider isn't showing as approved for our jail.**
- Providers must be enrolled in Veridian's network **and** approved by your
  jail administrator. Check **Settings → Providers** for approval status.

## Inmate kiosk setup

**The device can't connect to Veridian Health.**
- Verify outbound HTTPS (port 443) is allowed on the jail network.
- Confirm the device meets the setup checklist from onboarding.
- Run a test assessment with Veridian support before scheduling inmates.

**The camera isn't working.**
- Grant camera permissions to the browser or app on the facility device.
- Confirm no other application is using the camera.
- Test with the device's native camera app to isolate hardware vs. software.

## Photo capture issues

**Photos are rejected or flagged as low quality.**
- Ensure adequate lighting in the assessment location.
- Follow on-screen positioning prompts — all four angles (front, back, left,
  right) must be clearly visible.
- Have custody staff confirm the inmate can hold positions safely per jail
  policy.
- Retake individual photos using the in-app retry option.

**The inmate can't complete all four photos.**
- Document mobility limitations in the referral; some positions may need
  modification.
- If the inmate cannot safely complete capture, cancel the assessment and
  refer for in-person evaluation.

**Questionnaire won't advance.**
- Check for required fields marked in the UI.
- Confirm network connectivity hasn't dropped mid-session.
- If stuck, cancel and restart the assessment; partial data may not process.

## Provider dashboard

**Recommendations haven't appeared after completion.**
- Processing typically completes within minutes. Check the assessment status
  in the dashboard.
- If status shows "processing" for more than 30 minutes, contact support
  with the assessment ID.

**Recommendations include a red-flag alert.**
- This is expected behavior for certain presentations. Follow your jail's
  acute-care protocol — do not rely on the platform for emergency response.

**We need to amend or re-run an assessment.**
- Contact [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) with the
  assessment ID. Re-assessments can be initiated from the dashboard when
  clinically appropriate.

## Still stuck?

Email [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) with:

- Your jail name
- Assessment or referral ID (if applicable)
- A short description of the issue

For security concerns, email
[security@veridianhp.com](mailto:security@veridianhp.com) directly.
