# Troubleshooting

Most issues fall into a few categories: inmate PIN login, staff MFA access,
kiosk setup, photo capture, provider dashboard workflow, and messaging. Start
with the symptom that matches your situation.

## Inmate kiosk — login and session

**The inmate can't log in (number or PIN rejected).**
- Confirm the patient was enrolled and the number + PIN were handed to custody
  staff correctly.
- Check for typos — inmate numbers and PINs are case-sensitive where applicable.
- Ask an **admin** or enrolling staff member to **reset the PIN** from the
  provider dashboard.
- After several failed attempts, wait for any lockout period to expire or
  contact [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com).

**The session reset unexpectedly mid-intake.**
- Locked kiosks use **idle auto-reset** to clear inmate state. Have the inmate
  log in again and restart if needed.
- Inmate session state is in-memory only — a reset clears unsaved progress.

**The inmate sees only confirmation, not an exercise plan.**
- This is expected until a PT marks the submission **Reviewed** and releases
  the plan. Check status under **My plan / progress** (Received → Awaiting
  assignment → Under review → Reviewed).

## Staff dashboard — login and MFA

**I can't log in to the dashboard.**
- Confirm you're using the email address provisioned by an administrator.
- Reset your password from the login page.
- Ensure your **authenticator app** TOTP code is current (clock sync on your
  device).

**MFA / authenticator isn't working.**
- Re-sync time on your phone or hardware token.
- If you've lost the device, an **admin** can perform a **break-glass MFA
  reset** — you must re-enroll MFA on next login.
- Contact [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) if admin
  reset is unavailable.

**A staff member can't see cases or recommendations.**
- Check their **role** (`pt`, `admin`, `officer`, `warden`, `it_admin`) —
  clinical review requires `pt` (or admin) and **case assignment**.
- PTs only see submissions assigned to them unless their role allows broader
  access. See [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access).

## Kiosk setup and connectivity

**The device can't connect to Veridian Motion.**
- Verify outbound HTTPS (port 443) is allowed on the facility network.
- Confirm the PWA is installed and pointed at the correct environment.
- Run a test assessment with Veridian support before scheduling inmates.

**External links or apps open from the kiosk.**
- The app blocks external navigation; if links still open, verify **MDM**
  lockdown on the device — app hardening is not a substitute for agency MDM.
  See [Deployment and kiosk](/veridian-motion/deployment-and-kiosk).

## Photo capture

**The camera isn't working.**
- Grant camera permissions to the browser or installed PWA.
- Confirm no other application is using the camera.
- Test with the device's native camera app to isolate hardware vs. software.

**Photos are rejected or hard to align.**
- Ensure adequate lighting in the assessment location.
- Follow on-screen **silhouette overlays** for all four views (front, back,
  left, right).
- Retake individual views using the in-app retry option.

**Voice-to-text for the complaint isn't available.**
- Voice input depends on browser and device support; typed complaint is always
  available.

**The inmate can't complete all four photos.**
- Document mobility limitations in the complaint text.
- If capture isn't safe, cancel and refer for in-person evaluation per jail
  policy.

## Provider dashboard workflow

**A submission isn't appearing in my queue.**
- New submissions land in the **unassigned** queue until an **admin** assigns
  a PT.
- Confirm AI processing completed — status should advance from **Received**.

**I see red-flag alerts on a case.**
- Review red flags **first** before approving any plan. Follow your facility's
  acute-care protocol — the platform supports triage, not emergency response.

**PDF export or email failed.**
- PDF export generates a plain assessment summary for allowlisted addresses
  only.
- Email to external addresses requires a **BAA-covered** provider configured
  for your deployment. Contact your administrator or Veridian support.

**Secure messaging isn't delivering.**
- Messaging is internal only (append-only, monitored, part of the medical
  record). There is no external egress — inmates and staff both use in-app
  threads.

## Still stuck?

Email [support@veridianhp.com](mailto:support@veridianhp.com) with:

- Your facility name
- Submission or patient ID (if applicable)
- Role and surface (kiosk vs. dashboard)
- A short description of the issue

For security concerns, email
[security@veridianhp.com](mailto:security@veridianhp.com) directly.
