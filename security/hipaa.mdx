# HIPAA

Veridian Health Partners processes Protected Health Information (PHI) on
behalf of covered entities — correctional healthcare programs and their
providers. Veridian operates as a **Business Associate** under HIPAA when a
signed **BAA** is in place.

<Warning>
  **HIPAA-aligned design is not HIPAA certification.** Veridian Motion is
  not certified under HIPAA. Compliance depends on BAAs, agency hosting
  choices, and operational practices.
</Warning>

## What this means in practice

- A **BAA** is required before production PHI flows
- Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards align with the HIPAA
  Security Rule intent (45 CFR Part 164, Subpart C)
- Breach notification procedures follow the HIPAA Breach Notification Rule
  (45 CFR Subpart D) when applicable
- Subprocessors that touch PHI hold BAAs with Veridian Health Partners

## PHI in Veridian Motion

PHI includes, among other items:

- Inmate identifiers and enrollment records
- Assessment complaints, body-map selections, and Q&A
- Posture photos
- AI triage output, PT notes, and approved exercise plans
- Internal messages between care team and inmate

Veridian Motion collects the **minimum necessary** for MSK triage workflow.

## Minimum necessary

- Inmates see only their own data; plans only after PT review; notes only if shared
- Staff access is scoped by role and assignment (RLS)
- Audit logs record actions without copying clinical content

## Patient rights

Inmate HIPAA rights (access, amendment, accounting of disclosures) are
exercised through the **covered entity / agency**, not directly with Veridian.
Veridian supplies data to authorized agency staff to fulfill requests.

## Corrections-specific note

HIPAA applies to covered healthcare functions in custody settings. **CJIS
security policy**, **FedRAMP**, and **state corrections IT standards** are
separate frameworks — **not built into Veridian Motion**. Agencies host and
operate the product in environments that satisfy their obligations.

## Subprocessors

PHI may be processed by subprocessors including:

- Lovable Cloud / Supabase (database, auth, storage)
- LLM providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, xAI, fallback gateway) — server-side only
- Allowlisted email provider (when PDF email is enabled — BAA required)

Current subprocessor list available to customers on request.
