  - Tailored questionnaire
  - AI-assisted analysis with PT-driven recommendations

  **Provider model:**
  - PTs enroll in Veridian's network
  - Each jail approves providers independently

  **Scope:**
  - MVP is assessment + recommendations, not live video consults
  - Veridian Billing marked as future product with no timeline
  - All jails that purchase the product are supported
