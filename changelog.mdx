<Update label="Veridian Motion — product documentation" description="2026-07-05">
  Renamed product from Veridian Health / PostureCare to **Veridian Motion**.
  Removed discontinued Veridian Billing content. Documentation now reflects
  the built product:

  - [Overview](/veridian-motion/overview) — two surfaces, MVP scope, PT-review gate
  - [Inmate kiosk flow](/veridian-motion/inmate-kiosk-flow) — PIN login, body map,
    four-view capture, status-only until review
  - [Provider dashboard](/veridian-motion/provider-dashboard) — roster, enrollment,
    assignment queue, review workflow, PDF/email, messaging
  - [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access) — five roles, staff MFA,
    break-glass reset
  - [AI and clinical safety](/veridian-motion/ai-and-clinical-safety) — multi-LLM
    router, non-diagnostic output, model transparency
  - [Data model & privacy](/veridian-motion/data-model-privacy) — RLS, signed URLs,
    audit log
  - [Deployment and kiosk](/veridian-motion/deployment-and-kiosk) — PWA hardening vs
    agency MDM

  **Product highlights in this release:**
  - Inmate **number + PIN** authentication (staff-provisioned, no self sign-up)
  - Staff **email + password + TOTP MFA**
  - **Multi-LLM router** (Claude → GPT → Grok → fallback gateway) with failover
  - **Secure internal messaging** (append-only, monitored)
  - **PWA / kiosk hardening** (CSP, no external nav, idle reset, self-hosted fonts)
  - **USWDS-aligned** government UI redesign
  - Honest compliance framing — HIPAA-aligned design, not certified; agency-provided
    CJIS/FedRAMP/MDM
</Update>
