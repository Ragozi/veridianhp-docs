# Getting started

This checklist helps **administrators and IT** bring Veridian Motion online at
a correctional facility. For manual enrollment, staff MFA, and deployment
detail, see [Facility onboarding & integration](/veridian-motion/facility-onboarding).

<Note>
  Use **synthetic or de-identified data** in pilot until BAAs and agency
  hosting requirements are satisfied.
</Note>

## Phase 1 — Contract and compliance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execute agreements">
    Sign facility agreement with Veridian Health Partners. Execute a **BAA**
    before production PHI flows.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm hosting posture">
    The **hosting agency** provides CJIS/FedRAMP/GovCloud environment if
    required. Veridian Motion is **HIPAA-aligned, not HIPAA-certified**; **not
    CJIS-authorized or FedRAMP-authorized**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Identify contacts">
    Designate agency contacts for clinical operations, IT, and security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 2 — IT and kiosk setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Network">
    Allow outbound **HTTPS (443)** to the Veridian Motion app host and backend
    endpoints per deployment documentation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the PWA">
    Install Veridian Motion on kiosk devices via **Add to Home Screen /
    Install**. See [Deployment and kiosk](/veridian-motion/deployment-and-kiosk).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply MDM lockdown">
    Configure agency **MDM** for supervised kiosk mode. App hardening alone is
    not sufficient.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test connectivity">
    Run a test sign-in and photo capture before patient use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 3 — Staff accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create staff accounts">
    An **admin** provisions staff emails — local accounts (no SSO in current release).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enroll TOTP MFA">
    Staff complete **TOTP MFA** enrollment if required for the deployment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant roles">
    At `/staff`, assign `pt`, `admin`, or `it_admin`. See
    [Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 4 — Clinical readiness

<Steps>
  <Step title="Train PTs">
    Cover [Provider dashboard](/veridian-motion/provider-dashboard) and
    [AI and clinical safety](/veridian-motion/ai-and-clinical-safety) —
    red-flag-first review and the PT-approval gate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Train custody staff">
    Kiosk escort, PIN handoff, idle auto-reset, **see medical staff** safety
    guidance. See [Patient portal](/veridian-motion/patient-portal).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 5 — First live case

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enroll a patient">
    Admin uses **Enroll patient** → record **Patient ID + initial PIN** → hand
    to custody staff.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient intake">
    Patient signs in on the kiosk, completes body map, complaint, questionnaire,
    and up to four photos, then submits.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign and approve">
    Admin assigns from the queue to a PT. PT reviews, edits plan, **approves**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm patient release">
    Patient opens **My plan / progress** and confirms **Reviewed** status shows
    the approved plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>
