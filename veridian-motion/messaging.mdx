# Messaging

Veridian Motion includes **secure clinician-to-patient messaging** — an
**append-only** thread between staff and patients inside the application. It
supports follow-up after assessment without SMS, email, or external links.

<Warning>
  Messaging is **monitored** and **append-only**. It is part of the medical
  record.
</Warning>

## Design rules

| Rule | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Internal only** | No SMS, email to patients, or external URLs |
| **Append-only** | Messages cannot be edited or deleted after send |
| **Monitored** | Displayed as part of the medical record |
| **No external egress** | No outbound delivery outside the app |
| **RLS-scoped** | Patients see only their own threads; PTs see assigned patients |

## Who can message

| Role | Access |
| --- | --- |
| **pt** | Message assigned patients |
| **admin** | Operational messaging per policy — not a substitute for PT review |
| **Patient** | Read and reply within own threads on the kiosk |

## Where messaging lives

**Staff** — from the **patient record** or **assessment review** screen in
the provider dashboard.

**Patients** — from the kiosk alongside **My plan / progress**, within active
care threads.

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="PT reviews assessment">
    After intake, the assigned PT reviews the AI plan and intake data.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clinician sends a message">
    The PT (or authorized staff) sends a plain-language message in the secure
    thread. The patient sees the monitored-record notice.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient responds on kiosk">
    The patient reads and replies during a supervised session. Replies are
    append-only.
  </Step>

  <Step title="PT completes follow-up">
    The PT updates the assessment or plan if needed and **approves** when
    ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What messaging is not

- **Not live chat** — asynchronous only
- **Not video or phone** — not in the current release
- **Not external email** — PDF export is a separate staff tool
- **Not private or ephemeral** — retained per agency policy

## Audit

Message sends are recorded in the **hash-chained audit log** (action type and
resource ID — not message body). See
[Data model & privacy](/veridian-motion/data-model-privacy).

<Note>
  **Rolling out:** message safety screening (custody/safety escalation, blocked
  threats/contraband content) and an admin-only **Communication Record** (PDF
  export, audit-logged access). Not yet documented as available in all
  deployments — see [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
</Note>
