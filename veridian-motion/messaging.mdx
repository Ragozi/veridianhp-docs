# Messaging

Veridian Motion includes **secure clinician-to-patient messaging** — an
**append-only** thread inside the application. It supports follow-up after
assessment without SMS, email, or external links.

<Warning>
  Messaging is **monitored**, **append-only**, and part of the **medical
  record**. Safety screening applies to message content — see below.
</Warning>

## Design rules

| Rule | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Internal only** | No SMS, email to patients, or external URLs |
| **Append-only** | Messages cannot be edited or deleted after send |
| **RLS-scoped** | Patients see only their own threads; PTs see assigned patients |
| **Safety screening** | Messages are screened for custody and safety concerns (see below) |

## Message safety screening

Patient ↔ clinician messages are **screened for safety** before delivery:

| Signal | System behavior |
| --- | --- |
| **Self-harm indicators** | **Escalated to facility staff in real time** for follow-up per facility protocol |
| **Threats or contraband-related content** | **Blocked** — not delivered |

<Note>
  This is **operational safety screening** for secure messaging in a
  correctional healthcare workflow — **not** a psychiatric or behavioral-health
  assessment module and **not** AI emotional support.
</Note>

## Communication Record

An **admin-only**, **immutable** **Communication Record** captures messaging
threads for records or legal use:

- Exportable to **PDF**
- **Every access** to the Communication Record is recorded in the **hash-chained audit log**
- **`warden`** and **`pt`** roles do **not** access the Communication Record by default

## Who can message

| Role | Access |
| --- | --- |
| **pt** | Message assigned patients (clinical follow-up) |
| **admin** | Operational messaging per policy; **Communication Record** access |
| **Patient** | Read and reply in own threads on the kiosk |

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clinician sends a message">
    The PT sends a plain-language message in the secure thread. Content passes
    safety screening.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient responds on kiosk">
    The patient reads and replies during a supervised session. Replies are
    screened and append-only.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Escalation if needed">
    If screening triggers escalation, **facility staff** follow custody and
    clinical protocols — the platform does not dispatch emergency services.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Records access">
    An **admin** may export the **Communication Record** to PDF when authorized;
    access is audit-logged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What messaging is not

- **Not live chat** — asynchronous only
- **Not video or phone**
- **Not external email**
- **Not a psychological or mood assessment** tool

## Audit

Message sends and **Communication Record** access are recorded in the
**hash-chained audit log** (action type and resource ID — not message body in
audit payload). See [Data model & privacy](/veridian-motion/data-model-privacy).
