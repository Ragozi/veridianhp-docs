# Veridian Motion

**Veridian Motion** is the flagship product of **Veridian Health Partners** — a
physical therapy platform built for **correctional facilities**. It connects
**patient portal intake** on a locked kiosk with **licensed PT review** on a
provider dashboard, so musculoskeletal complaints get structured assessment,
AI-assisted non-diagnostic triage, and **PT-approved exercise recommendations**.

## Why Veridian Motion exists

Correctional facilities handle high volumes of musculoskeletal complaints —
often with limited on-site PT time. Veridian Motion gives clinical and custody
staff a repeatable workflow:

- Patients report **what hurts**, complete a **questionnaire**, and capture
  **up to four guided posture photos** on a facility-controlled kiosk.
- AI produces an **immediate PT-reviewable exercise plan** (name, why, steps,
  dosage, cautions) — **never shown to the patient before PT approval**.
- The PT retains **full clinical authority**, edits the plan, and **approves**
  when ready.

## Facility impact

Structured MSK triage and **PT-approved movement plans** can improve **patient
wellbeing** and help facilities address complaints before they escalate into
costlier outcomes — emergency transfers, chronic disability, repeat sick calls,
and litigation exposure.

<Note>
  Veridian Motion is a **clinical workflow product** — not billing, EHR, live
  video, or partner API. Those are on the [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap)
  or out of scope.
</Note>

## Built for physical therapists

| PT capability | What it means in practice |
| --- | --- |
| **Assignment queue** | Admin assigns submissions to PTs |
| **Assessment review** | Body map, questionnaire, photos, AI plan |
| **Red-flag triage** | Review-first callouts; patient sees **see medical staff** — not diagnosis |
| **Exercise planning** | Edit AI plan: name, why, steps, dosage, cautions |
| **Approve and release** | Nothing reaches the patient until PT **approves** |
| **Messaging** | Secure clinician-to-patient thread |
| **PDF export** | Plain assessment PDF |

## How a case moves through the system

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enroll">
    An **admin** manually enrolls the patient and receives **Patient ID +
    initial PIN**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Intake on kiosk">
    The patient signs in, selects body-map regions, describes the complaint,
    completes the questionnaire, and captures up to four posture photos.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and wait">
    The patient sees **confirmation and safety guidance only**. AI generates a
    PT-reviewable plan in the dashboard — not on the kiosk.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign and review">
    An **admin** assigns a **PT** from the queue. The PT reviews red flags,
    intake, and the AI plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve">
    The PT **approves** the assessment. The patient may then view the approved
    plan and shared PT notes in **My plan / progress**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
