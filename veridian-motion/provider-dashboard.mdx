# Provider dashboard

The provider dashboard is the staff-facing surface of Veridian Motion. Licensed
PTs retain **clinical authority** — AI output is triage support only and does
not reach patients until **approved**.

## Sign-in and first login

Staff sign in with **email**, **password**, and **TOTP MFA** (available;
**enforced per deployment**). On first login, staff enroll an authenticator app
if MFA is required for their deployment. An **admin** grants roles at `/staff`
and may reset MFA (break-glass). See
[Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access).

## Patient roster

A **searchable, filterable patient roster** lists manually enrolled patients
for the **selected facility**. Staff with multi-facility access **switch
facilities** before opening clinical records. **`warden`** users do not receive
clinical roster views — operational metrics only. See
[Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions).

## Enroll patient (manual)

Today, enrollment is **one patient at a time**:

1. An **admin** uses **Enroll patient** to create the patient record
2. The system returns **Patient ID** and **initial PIN** for staff handoff
3. The patient completes intake on the kiosk

Staff **reset PINs** from the patient record when needed. Bulk CSV import and
JMS sync are **planned** — see [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

For deployment and onboarding, see
[Facility onboarding & integration](/veridian-motion/facility-onboarding).

## Assignment queue

New submissions appear in an **assignment queue** until an **admin assigns a
PT**. See [Status and workflow](/veridian-motion/status-and-workflow).

## Assessment review and approve

Opening an assessment shows:

- **Body-map selections**, complaint, and **questionnaire** responses
- **Posture photos** (up to four views)
- **AI-generated exercise plan** — name, why, steps, dosage, cautions — for PT
  review (non-diagnostic)
- **Red-flag callouts** — **review first** in the dashboard; patients may see
  calm **see medical staff** guidance, never a diagnosis
- **PT notes** — mark **shared with patient** only when appropriate

The PT **approves** the assessment when ready. Until then, **nothing** from AI
or the draft plan is visible to the patient.

<Warning>
  Red-flag alerts are **AI-assisted triage support**, not a diagnosis or
  emergency dispatch system.
</Warning>

### Releasing the exercise plan

On **approve**, the patient may view the edited exercise plan in **My plan /
progress** — name, why, steps, dosage, cautions — plus any **shared PT notes**.

## Tools (built today)

### PDF export

Export a **plain PDF** of an assessment for records or handoff. Generation
occurs server-side.

### Secure messaging

**Clinician-to-patient messaging** — append-only, with **safety screening**
(self-harm escalated to facility staff in real time; threats/contraband
blocked). Admins may access the immutable **Communication Record** (PDF export;
every access audit-logged). See [Messaging](/veridian-motion/messaging).

### Admin analytics (rolling out)

A per-facility, **admin-only** engagement and outcomes dashboard — **measured**
vs **estimated** cost figures, no individual PHI. Confirm availability with
Veridian. See [Admin analytics](/veridian-motion/admin-analytics).
