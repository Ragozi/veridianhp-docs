# Platform overview

For product vision and case flow, start with
[Veridian Motion](/veridian-motion/product). This page lists what is **built
today**.

## What it is

| Surface | Users | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Patient portal (kiosk)** | Patients (Patient ID + PIN) | Body map, complaint, questionnaire, up to 4 photos, status and approved plan |
| **Provider dashboard** | Staff (`pt`, `admin`, `warden`, `it_admin`) | Roster, enrollment, assignment, review/approve, messaging, PDF, multi-facility |

TanStack Start web app (React 19), installable **PWA**, USWDS-aligned UI,
backed by Supabase (Postgres, Auth, Storage) via Lovable Cloud.

## Built today

**Patient portal**

- Sign-in with **Patient ID + PIN** (staff-provisioned)
- Body-map region selector, complaint, plain-language **questionnaire**
- Up to **four guided posture photos**
- Post-submit confirmation; **My plan / progress** status tracking
- Approved exercise plan and shared PT notes **only after PT approval**
- Red-flag inputs → calm **see medical staff** safety action (not diagnosis)

**Provider dashboard**

- Roles: `pt`, `admin`, `warden`, `it_admin`
- **Manual** patient enrollment
- **Multi-facility isolation** — RLS scoped by facility membership; staff can **switch facilities**
- **`warden`** — operational visibility only (counts, activity, safety escalations) — **no clinical PHI**
- **Assignment queue** → assessment **review/approve**
- PT notes, **PDF export**
- **Secure messaging** with **safety screening** and admin **Communication Record** (PDF, audit-logged access)

**AI**

- Non-diagnostic triage → immediate **PT-reviewable exercise plan** (name / why / steps / dosage / cautions)
- Licensed PT retains clinical authority

**Security**

- RBAC, RLS on every table (facility-scoped)
- Hash-chained audit log, field-level PHI encryption, brute-force lockout
- PWA/kiosk hardening; staff **TOTP MFA** (enforced per deployment)

## What it is not (current release)

- **Not a diagnosis tool**
- **Not live video, EHR, or partner API** — see [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap)
- **Not SSO, bulk roster import, or JMS integration**
- **Not HIPAA-certified, CJIS-authorized, or FedRAMP-authorized** — see [Security overview](/security/overview)
- **Not AI behavioral-health or emotional-assessment modules**

## PT-review authority

Nothing from AI reaches a patient until a licensed PT **approves** the assessment.
