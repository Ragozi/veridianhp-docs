# Admin analytics

<Warning>
  **Rolling out.** The admin analytics / ROI dashboard is not yet available in
  every deployment. Confirm with Veridian Health Partners before relying on
  these reports. See [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
</Warning>

Veridian Motion is adding a **per-facility, admin-only analytics dashboard** for
operational and outcomes reporting across the platform.

## What it shows

| Category | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Engagement** | Platform usage and workflow activity at the **facility level** |
| **Outcomes** | Aggregated indicators tied to the MSK triage and PT-review workflow |
| **Cost framing** | **Measured** metrics shown separately from clearly labeled **estimated** cost figures — no invented or guaranteed savings numbers |

## Privacy and minimum necessary

- **No individual patient PHI** on the analytics dashboard
- Staff-related wellness figures, when present, appear only in **aggregate /
  de-identified** form
- **`warden`** and other oversight roles do **not** receive this admin analytics
  view unless also granted `admin`

## What it is not

- **Not a clinical record** — reporting only
- **Not a billing or coding tool**
- **Not a behavioral-health or psychological assessment** product
- **Not a certification claim** — HIPAA-aligned platform design applies; this
  dashboard does not imply HIPAA certification or any other compliance certification
