# Officer & Staff Wellness

<Note>
  This page describes an **offering and approach** from Veridian Health
  Partners — **not** a certified, fully deployed, or finished product module.
  Availability varies by facility agreement. Contact
  [Veridian Health Partners](https://veridianhp.com/request-access) to discuss
  scope.
</Note>

Veridian Motion's core platform focuses on **patient** musculoskeletal triage
inside correctional facilities. Separately, Veridian Health Partners extends a
**movement-based, PT-reviewed philosophy** to support **correctional staff**
wellness — injury prevention, movement and mobility, shift recovery, and
general **stress-resilience** self-help content.

<Warning>
  This is **employee wellness**, not patient care. It is governed by **employee-
  wellness** frameworks (such as EEOC, ADA, and GINA considerations) — a
  **different regime** than patient HIPAA. Veridian Motion is **HIPAA-aligned,
  not HIPAA-certified**; this offering does **not** claim HIPAA certification
  or any other compliance certification for staff programs.
</Warning>

## Our approach

| Principle | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Voluntary** | Participation is **optional** for each staff member |
| **Confidential to the participant** | Individual activity is **not** exposed to the facility as identifiable records |
| **Aggregate facility insight only** | The facility may see **anonymous, aggregate** trends — not individual staff PHI or wellness scores tied to a person |
| **Non-diagnostic** | Movement guidance, self-tracking, and **referral to human support** — the offering does **not** diagnose conditions or provide therapy through the app |
| **Human support when needed** | Escalation paths point to **people** (EAP, occupational health, supervisors per policy) — not AI clinical or psychological assessment |

## What it includes (conceptually)

- **Injury prevention** and movement hygiene for demanding shift work
- **Mobility and recovery** content aligned with physical-therapy principles
- **Shift-recovery** routines staff can use off duty
- **Stress-resilience** self-help — general wellness education, **not** AI
  emotional assessment or mood screening

## What it explicitly is not

- **Not** patient/inmate care or a substitute for the patient portal workflow
- **Not** a psychiatric, psychological, or behavioral-health **assessment**
  product — and **not** an AI that evaluates a person's emotional or mental state
- **Not** a promise of specific health outcomes or dollar savings
- **Not** mandatory monitoring of individual officers by facility leadership

<Note>
  Veridian Health Partners does **not** document unreleased AI modules that
  assess mood, psychology, or emotional state. Public docs describe **staff
  wellness content and aggregate reporting** only.
</Note>
