# Facility onboarding & integration

Veridian Motion serves correctional facilities through two **distinct identity
populations**, each handled differently.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Staff">
    **Physical therapists, administrators, and IT admins** are named users tied
    to the facility workforce.

    **Built today:** Local **email + password** with **TOTP MFA** (enforced per
    deployment). Roles include `pt`, `admin`, `warden` (operational only), and
    `it_admin`. **Multi-facility** staff can switch facilities; RLS isolates each
    facility's data.

    **Planned:** Single sign-on via agency identity provider (SAML/OIDC). See
    [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Patients">
    Patients are **not** federated identities. Staff **manually enroll** each
    patient for their **facility** (manual, one at a time). Patients sign in with
    **Patient ID + PIN** on a locked, **MDM-managed** kiosk.

    Patient onboarding is **manual provisioning**, not SSO. Bulk CSV import and
    JMS sync are **planned** — see [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Provisioning patient accounts (built today)

Today, enrollment is **manual** (one patient at a time per facility):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Admin enrolls one patient">
    An administrator uses **Enroll patient** in the
    [provider dashboard](/veridian-motion/provider-dashboard) to create a
    single patient record for this facility.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Patient ID + PIN">
    The system returns a **Patient ID** and **initial PIN** for staff to hand
    to the patient through the facility's existing credential channel (e.g.,
    intake paperwork).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient completes intake">
    The patient signs in on the kiosk and completes the
    [patient portal](/veridian-motion/patient-portal) flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Planned:** Bulk CSV roster import and JMS/booking-system API integration
  for automated provisioning at scale. See [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
</Note>

## Credentials and PINs

- Patients authenticate with **Patient ID** and a **4–12 digit PIN**.
- PINs are **unique per patient**, generated at enrollment and delivered
  through the facility's existing channel, or set at first kiosk use after a
  staff-verified identity step.
- Staff may **reset a PIN** from the patient record.
- **Never** use a facility-wide shared PIN.

<Warning>
  **Brute-force lockout** protects both patient and staff logins. Repeated
  failed attempts temporarily lock that identifier.
</Warning>

## Staff access (built today)

Staff use **local accounts** with **TOTP MFA** (available; enforced per
deployment). An **admin** grants roles at `/staff`:

| Role | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `pt` | Clinical review, approve plans, notes, messaging (facility-scoped) |
| `admin` | Enrollment, assignment, roles, MFA reset, Communication Record |
| `warden` | **Operational only** — counts, activity, safety escalations; **no clinical PHI** |
| `it_admin` | Security settings, compliance views, technical administration |

See [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access) and
[Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions).

## Multi-facility isolation

Veridian Motion **isolates each facility's patients and staff** through **RLS**
scoped by **facility membership**:

- **`pt`** clinicians see clinical data for **their facility only**
- **`warden`** sees **operational** metrics and safety escalations — **no clinical PHI** (HIPAA minimum-necessary)
- Authorized staff can **switch between facilities** in the dashboard

See [Roles and access](/veridian-motion/roles-and-access).

## Deployment

### Kiosks

- **Facility-issued tablets** locked to Veridian Motion via agency **MDM**.
- No outside web browsing or external messaging from the kiosk.
- **True device lockdown** is provided by agency MDM; the app provides
  software-side PWA/kiosk hardening. See
  [Deployment and kiosk](/veridian-motion/deployment-and-kiosk).

### Hosting and compliance

Veridian Motion is **HIPAA-aligned, not HIPAA-certified**; it is **not
CJIS-authorized or FedRAMP-authorized**. The **hosting agency** provides
CJIS/FedRAMP/GovCloud environment, MDM kiosk lockdown, network controls, and
personnel screening per its security policy.

| Requirement | Owner |
| --- | --- |
| Signed **BAA** with the covered entity | Agency + Veridian Health Partners |
| **CJIS / FedRAMP / GovCloud** hosting (if required) | Agency |
| Network segregation | Agency |
| Personnel screening | Agency |
| Data classification review | Agency |

See [Security overview](/security/overview) and [HIPAA](/security/hipaa).

## Onboarding runbook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contract, BAA, and data-classification review">
    Execute facility agreement and BAA. Confirm hosting decision.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provision facilities">
    Veridian Health Partners provisions facility tenant(s) and **facility-scoped**
    configuration. Multi-facility agencies receive isolated RLS per site.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect staff identity">
    Create **local staff accounts** with **TOTP MFA** (per deployment policy).
    Assign roles (`pt`, `admin`, `warden`, `it_admin`) and facility membership.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enroll patients manually">
    Admins enroll patients one at a time until bulk import or JMS sync is
    available. See [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provision kiosks">
    Install the PWA via **MDM**. Issue Patient ID + PIN through facility process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Train staff and launch pilot">
    Train PTs on [review/approve workflow](/veridian-motion/provider-dashboard)
    and [AI and clinical safety](/veridian-motion/ai-and-clinical-safety).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor">
    Review the **hash-chained audit log** and operational reporting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
