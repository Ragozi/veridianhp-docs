# Roadmap

This page lists capabilities that are **not yet generally available** in every
deployment. For what is **built today**, see
[Platform overview](/veridian-motion/overview) and
[Patient portal](/veridian-motion/patient-portal).

<Warning>
  Treat **Rolling out** items as available only when your facility has been
  upgraded and Veridian Health Partners confirms activation. Until then,
  assume **single-facility** deployments and **basic** secure messaging only.
</Warning>

## Rolling out

Capabilities below are in active rollout. They are **not** documented as
available on current-capability pages until your deployment is confirmed live.

### Multi-facility isolation and facility oversight

| Item | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Facility-scoped data isolation** | Each facility's patients and staff are isolated through row-level security scoped by **facility membership** |
| **Clinical access (`pt`)** | PT/clinician sees clinical data for **their facility only** |
| **Facility oversight (`warden`)** | Operational visibility only — enrollment counts, confirmation that care workflows are active, and **safety escalations** — **no clinical PHI** (HIPAA minimum-necessary) |
| **Facility switching** | Staff with access to multiple facilities can **switch between facilities** in the dashboard |

<Note>
  The **`warden`** role is part of this rollout. It does **not** grant access to
  assessments, AI output, exercise plans, clinical notes, or message bodies.
</Note>

### Secure messaging — safety and records

| Item | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Message safety screening** | Clinician-to-patient messages are screened for custody and safety concerns. Qualifying content is **escalated to facility staff in real time**. **Threats and contraband-related content are blocked** |
| **Communication Record** | **Admin-only**, **immutable** record of messaging threads, exportable to **PDF** for records or legal use. **Every access** to the Communication Record is **audit-logged** |

<Note>
  This is **operational safety screening** for secure messaging — not a
  behavioral-health, psychiatric, or patient-facing emotional-support module.
</Note>

## Planned

| Capability | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Staff SSO (SAML / OIDC)** | Single sign-on via agency identity providers (e.g., Microsoft Entra ID, Google Workspace, Okta) with MFA and IdP group → Motion role mapping |
| **Bulk roster provisioning (CSV import)** | Admin upload of a roster CSV to create/update patients and issue PINs at scale |
| **JMS / booking-system integration** | Live or scheduled sync with jail management systems (e.g., Tyler, Spillman/Motorola Solutions, CentralSquare, Securus) — auto-provision on booking, deactivate on release |
| **Scheduled SFTP roster feed** | Daily roster file drop for automated reconcile |
| **EHR / EMR integration** | Automated clinical data exchange with external systems |
| **Partner API** | Programmatic access for third-party integrations |
| **Live video consults** | Synchronous telehealth sessions |

## Explicitly out of scope (current release)

- Medical billing / coding workflows
- AI output shown to patients before PT review
- Self-service patient or staff sign-up
- External messaging (SMS, email to patients)
- Behavioral-health, psychiatric, mood/wellness, or emotional-support AI modules
