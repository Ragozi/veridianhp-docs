# For evaluators

For **jail medical directors, physical therapists, IT, and compliance staff**
evaluating Veridian Motion. This page reflects **what is built today** only.
Planned items are on the [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

## What Veridian Motion is

Veridian Health Partners' **flagship PT platform** for **correctional
facilities**:

- **Patient portal (kiosk)** — body map, complaint, questionnaire, up to four
  posture photos (Patient ID + PIN)
- **Provider dashboard** — assignment queue, assessment review/**approve**, PT
  notes, PDF export, secure messaging

AI produces **non-diagnostic**, **PT-reviewable** exercise plans (name / why /
steps / dosage / cautions). A licensed PT **approves** before anything reaches
the patient.

## Why facilities consider it

Structured MSK triage and PT-approved movement care can improve **patient
wellbeing** and reduce escalations that drive emergency care, chronic
disability, and litigation costs — while keeping clinical authority with
on-site licensed PTs.

## How it works (built today)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Admin enrolls patient manually">
    Single-facility, one-at-a-time enrollment → **Patient ID + PIN** for staff
    handoff.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient completes portal intake">
    Body map → complaint → questionnaire → up to four photos → submit. Patient
    sees **confirmation only** — no AI plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI generates PT-reviewable plan">
    Server-side, non-diagnostic plan appears in the dashboard for PT review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Admin assigns PT">
    Submission moves through the **assignment queue**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="PT approves">
    PT reviews red flags, edits plan, **approves**. Patient sees plan in **My
    plan / progress**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Limitations

- **Not diagnostic** — triage support only; PT retains authority
- **Not emergency response** — red flags prompt **see medical staff**; facility protocols govern urgent care
- **HIPAA-aligned, not HIPAA-certified**; **not CJIS/FedRAMP-authorized**
- **Agency provides** CJIS/FedRAMP/GovCloud hosting, MDM, network, personnel screening
- **Limited offline** — submit requires connectivity
- **AI can be wrong** — PT review mandatory
