# For evaluators

This page is for **jail medical directors, physical therapists, custody
leadership, IT, and compliance staff** deciding whether Veridian Motion fits
your facility. It summarizes what the product does today, how it works, what it
does **not** include, and where to read more.

## What Veridian Motion is

**Veridian Motion** is Veridian Health Partners' flagship **physical therapy
platform** for correctional facilities. It connects:

- An **inmate kiosk** — structured MSK intake (body map, complaint, four
  posture photos)
- A **provider dashboard** — licensed PT review, exercise planning, messaging,
  and controlled release to the inmate

AI assists PTs with **non-diagnostic triage support**. A licensed PT must
**review and approve** everything before it reaches an inmate. The product
does **not** diagnose.

## Why facilities consider it

Correctional settings see high volumes of musculoskeletal complaints — back,
shoulder, knee, and posture-related issues — often with limited on-site PT
time. Untreated or poorly triaged MSK problems can escalate into costlier
outcomes: emergency transfers, prolonged disability, repeat sick calls, and
in some cases litigation.

Veridian Motion aims to improve **inmate wellbeing** through early, structured
assessment and **PT-reviewed bodyweight exercise plans** delivered inside
custody — without replacing in-person care when red flags warrant it.

<Note>
  Veridian Motion does **not** include medical billing, coding, EHR
  integration, live video visits, or partner API connectivity in the current
  release. Those capabilities may be considered in future phases.
</Note>

## How it works (summary)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Staff enroll the inmate">
    An admin creates the account and receives an **inmate number + PIN** for
    custody handoff.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Inmate completes kiosk intake">
    Body map → complaint (optional voice-to-text) → four-view photos → submit.
    The inmate sees **confirmation only** — no AI output.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Server processes the assessment">
    AI triage support is generated server-side (multi-LLM router with failover).
    Output appears in the staff dashboard only.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Admin assigns a PT">
    New submissions sit in an **unassigned queue** until assigned.
  </Step>

  <Step title="PT reviews and releases">
    Red flags first, then photos, AI observations, draft plan. PT edits the
    plan, adds notes, marks **reviewed**. Approved content appears in the
    inmate's **My plan / progress** view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What is not in scope today

| Not included | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Live video consults | Asynchronous workflow only |
| EHR / EMR integration | No automated feed to external clinical systems |
| Partner API | No public API for third-party connectivity yet |
| Medical billing / coding | Separate product line discontinued; not part of Veridian Motion |
| Self sign-up | Staff provision all accounts |
| AI direct to inmate | PT-review gate on all clinical output |

## Limitations (read before pilot)

- **Not diagnostic** — AI provides triage support; PT retains clinical authority
- **Not emergency response** — red flags prompt review; facility protocols govern urgent care
- **Not HIPAA-certified** — HIPAA-aligned design; BAA required for production PHI
- **Not CJIS-authorized or FedRAMP-authorized** — agency provides compliant hosting and MDM
- **Limited offline** — submit and photo upload require network connectivity
- **AI can be wrong** — models may miss or mischaracterize; PT review is mandatory
