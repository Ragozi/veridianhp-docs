# Roles and access

Veridian Motion uses **role-based access control (RBAC)** and **row-level
security (RLS)** on every table, scoped by **facility membership**. Staff
roles are granted **only by an admin**.

## Multi-facility access

Each facility's **patients and staff** are isolated through RLS. Staff who
work across sites can **switch between facilities** in the dashboard after an
admin grants the appropriate roles and facility membership.

| Scope | Who | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Clinical (`pt`)** | Assigned PT/clinician | Clinical data for **their facility only** |
| **Operational (`warden`)** | Facility oversight | Counts, workflow activity, safety escalations — **no clinical PHI** |
| **Admin (`admin`)** | Facility administrator | Full operational + clinical admin for assigned facilities |

See [Role permissions](/veridian-motion/role-permissions) for the full matrix.

## Staff roles

| Role | ID | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Physical Therapist** | `pt` | Assigned assessments — review, approve, notes, messaging (facility-scoped) |
| **Administrator** | `admin` | Enrollment, assignment, roles, MFA reset, roster, Communication Record |
| **Warden / facility oversight** | `warden` | **Operational only** — no clinical PHI (minimum-necessary) |
| **IT Admin** | `it_admin` | Security settings, compliance views, technical administration |

<Note>
  Patient access is separate: patients authenticate with **Patient ID + PIN**.
  Patients see only their own submissions and approved/shared content.
</Note>

## Staff authentication

Staff accounts use:

1. **Email + password**
2. **TOTP MFA** — available on all staff accounts, **enforced per deployment**

Staff SSO (SAML/OIDC) is **planned** — see [Roadmap](/veridian-motion/roadmap).

## Role provisioning

Admins manage roles at **`/staff`**:

- **Grant** — assign `pt`, `admin`, `warden`, or `it_admin`
- **Revoke** — remove a role; access ends on next session
- Configure **facility membership** for multi-facility staff

Role changes are recorded in the **hash-chained audit log**.

## Break-glass MFA reset

An **admin** or **it_admin** may **reset MFA** when a device is lost. The user
must re-enroll at next sign-in.

## Patient credentials

| Action | Who | Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Enroll patient** | `admin` | Patient ID + initial PIN returned to staff |
| **Reset patient PIN** | `admin` | New PIN shown once for handoff |
| **Sign in** | Patient at kiosk | Access to own submissions only |
